Make maps of your journey

The unique map function of Bonvoyaj allows you to incorporate different kinds of maps in your diary, to show people where you’ve been. You can add two types of maps to your journal.

Share your Journey

Add stories to your digital diary and determine for each chapter if you want share it or not. Public chapters are converted to a blog that you can share with family and friends.

Add Photos
